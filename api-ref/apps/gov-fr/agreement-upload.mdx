---
title: "Upload signed agreement"
openapi: "POST /apps/gov-fr/v1/entry/{silo_entry_id}/agreement"
---

Submits the customer's signed agreement together with the chosen signing method. Two methods are supported: `esignature` validates an electronically-signed (PAdES) PDF cryptographically against the EU Trusted List; `identity` accepts a hand-signed PDF and defers verification to Invopop's approval service — uploaded identity images are required first.

Side-effects: the signed PDF is attached to the silo entry, the approval decision is recorded, and the queued `contract.wait.approval` task is poked so the onboarding workflow can continue.
