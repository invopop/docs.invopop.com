  <Accordion title="¿ What are the legal obligations for receiving invoices in Spain?">
    Recipients must retain invoices for 4 years (or 6 for accounting purposes). From 2027/2028 the Crea y Crece mandate adds receiving obligations for B2B: businesses must accept structured e-invoices and report status (acceptance, rejection, payment date) back to the issuer.
