  <Accordion title="Who is required to report through SII?">
    SII (_Suministro Inmediato de Información_) is mandatory for taxpayers that file VAT on a monthly basis. In practice this includes companies with annual turnover above €6,010,121.04, members of VAT groups (REGE), and businesses registered in the monthly VAT refund register (REDEME). Other taxpayers can opt in voluntarily.
  </Accordion>
