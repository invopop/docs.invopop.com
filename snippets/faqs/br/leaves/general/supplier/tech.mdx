  <Accordion title="¿ How are supplier credentials stored in Invopop for Brazil?">
    A1 certificates (X.509 + private key, exported as PKCS#12) and their passwords are stored encrypted at rest. Invopop uses them to sign documents and authenticate to the relevant SEFAZ or municipal endpoint on the supplier's behalf.
