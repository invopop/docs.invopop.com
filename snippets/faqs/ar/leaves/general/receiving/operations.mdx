  <Accordion title="¿ How do I receive invoices in Argentina?">
    Argentina's clearance model is one-way — ARCA does not deliver invoices to recipients. Suppliers send the authorized invoice (with CAE) to the buyer directly via email or another channel. To process them in Invopop, route the incoming PDF or XML through an Import workflow.
