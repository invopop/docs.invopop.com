  <Accordion title="How do I receive invoices in Argentina?">
    Argentina's clearance model is one-way — ARCA does not deliver invoices to recipients. Suppliers send the authorized invoice (with CAE) to the buyer directly via email or another channel. To process them in Invopop, you could route the incoming XML through an Import workflow. Get in touch with us if you are interested in managing your Accounts Payable in Argentina.
