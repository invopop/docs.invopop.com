  <Accordion title="Are there legal requirements for archiving received invoices in Argentina?">
    ARCA requires both issuers and recipients to retain invoices for 10 years (Article 48 RG 1415/03). Invopop preserves the original XML and any generated PDF in the silo entry for at least the required time.
