## Overview

Convert GOBL invoices into the German XRechnung format.

## How to use

This step must be preceded by "Generate PDF".

Output format:

* CII (Cross Industry Invoice)
* UBL (Unviersal Business Language)

The action will convert GOBL into the CII format (XML) and attach it to the human-readable PDF/A-3.
This step doesn't require configuration. 

## Availability

* Invoice workflows
* ~Parties workflows~
* ~Receipts workflows~
* ~Rescue workflows~
