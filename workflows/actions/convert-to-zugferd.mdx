## Overview

Convert GOBL invoices into the German ZUGFeRD XML format ready to be inserted into a PDF.

## How to use

This step must be preceded by "Generate PDF". 
The action will convert GOBL into the CII format (XML) and attach it to the human-readable PDF/A-3.
This step doesn't require configuration. 

## Availability

* Invoice workflows
* ~Parties workflows~
* ~Receipts workflows~
* ~Rescue workflows~
