## Overview

This action verifies that a EU VAT exists using the [VIES](https://europa.eu/youreurope/business/taxation/vat/check-vat-number-vies/index_en.htm) (VAT Information Exchange System) search engine owned by the European Comission.

## How to use

This workflow action can validate the Supplier and/or Customer VAT numbers. Select your desired validations in the "Validate Invoice Fields" dropdown. 

## Availability

* Invoice workflows
* Parties workflows
* Receipts workflows
* ~Rescue workflows~
