---
title: "Disable members"
description: "Disable or remove a member's access to your workspace."
sidebarTitle: "Disable"
mode: "wide"
---

To disable a member, click on the <kbd>Disable</kbd> button on the member's row. You will be prompted to confirm the action.

<Frame>
	<img src="/assets/admin/members-list.png" />
</Frame>

The member will be removed from your organization and will no longer have access to your workspaces.

If you need to re-enable a member, click on the **Deactivated members** tab and click on the <kbd>Enable</kbd> button on the member's row. You will be prompted to confirm the action.
