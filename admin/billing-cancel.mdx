To cancel your subscription, click on the <kbd>Cancel subscription</kbd> button in the top right corner of the billing page. You will be prompted to confirm the action.

<Frame>
	<img src="/assets/admin/billing-cancel.png" />
</Frame>

Your subscription will be cancelled immediately and you will no longer have access to Invopop.

If you change your mind, you can re-subscribe at any time.
