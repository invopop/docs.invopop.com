<Update label="May 7, 2026" tags={["new", "updates", "fixes"]}>

## New features

**France Plateforme Agréée (PA) support.** Send and receive electronic invoices on the French network ahead of the 2026 e-invoicing reform. Includes registration, invoicing, status reporting, and lifecycle reporting flows. See the [France PA guide](/guides/fr-pa).

**NO VERI\*FACTU for Spain.** A new compliance mode for Spanish suppliers who report through SII without using VERI\*FACTU. Walks through supplier onboarding and invoice issuing end to end. See the [NO VERI\*FACTU guide](/guides/es-noverifactu).

**IEET (Catalonia tourist tax) examples.** New invoice patterns for handling the Catalan tourist tax (IEET) in VERI\*FACTU and SII. The charge is modelled outside the VAT base while still flowing into the invoice total. See the [VERI\*FACTU](/guides/es-verifactu) and [SII](/guides/es-sii) guides.

**List all jobs endpoint.** A new `GET /transform/jobs` endpoint to fetch all transform jobs in a workspace. See the [API reference](/api-ref/transform/jobs/fetch-all-jobs).

## Updates

**Workflow editor refresh.** Updated UI for building and editing workflows, including a clearer template loader, restructured conditions and exceptions, and refreshed run controls. See the [workflows guide](/guides/workflows).

**Chargebee email integration.** Refreshed setup steps for sending Chargebee invoices through Invopop's email app. See the [Chargebee guide](/guides/chargebee).

## Removed

**InvoiceXpress (Portugal) integration.** The InvoiceXpress app has been retired. Portuguese suppliers should use the [AT Portugal app](/apps/at-portugal) for SAF-T reporting and series registration.

</Update>
