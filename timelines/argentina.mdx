## Timeline

Current and upcoming regulation for Argentina.

<Update label="Now" tags={["B2B", "B2G", "B2C"]}>
  **E-invoicing and e-reporting systems enforced to date**<br />
  Existing systems enforced in Argentina regarding e-invoicing, e-reporting and fiscalization.

  | System        | Description  | Invopop Coverage |
  |---------------|---------------|---------------|
  | [Factura Electrónica](#factura-electronica) | Argentina's mandatory electronic invoicing system, used to record, validate, and report all taxable transactions to ARCA in real time through a clearance model. | In development (Q1 2026) |

</Update>

<Update label="15 Apr 2025" tags={["B2B", "B2C"]}>
  **Foreign Currency E-Invoicing - Web Service Implementation**<br />
  Mandatory use of WebService technical manuals for [General Resolution 5616/2024](https://www.globalvatcompliance.com/globalvatnews/argentina-e-invoicing-procedures/) becomes effective. This resolution requires exchange rate information to be included in electronic invoices of types A, B, C, and E for transactions conducted in foreign currencies.
</Update>

<Update label="1 Apr 2025" tags={["B2B", "B2C"]}>
  **Consumer Tax Transparency Regime - All Taxpayers**<br />
  [General Resolution 5614/2024](https://www.argentina.gob.ar/normativa/nacional/resoluci%C3%B3n-5614-2024-407183) requires all remaining taxpayers (not classified as large companies) to itemize VAT and other indirect national taxes on electronic invoices.
</Update>

<Update label="5 Mar 2025" tags={["B2B", "B2C"]}>
  **Foreign Currency E-Invoicing - Mobile Systems Update**<br />
  Mobile Invoicing system and electronic receipt issuance platforms updated to support [General Resolution 5616/2024](https://www.globalvatcompliance.com/globalvatnews/argentina-e-invoicing-procedures/) foreign currency transaction requirements.
</Update>

<Update label="15 Jan 2025" tags={["B2B", "B2C"]}>
  **Foreign Currency E-Invoicing - Web Service Manuals Available**<br />
  WebService technical manuals for [General Resolution 5616/2024](https://www.globalvatcompliance.com/globalvatnews/argentina-e-invoicing-procedures/) become available for implementation. Online Vouchers service becomes available for use.
</Update>

<Update label="1 Jan 2025" tags={["B2B", "B2C"]}>
  **Consumer Tax Transparency Regime - Large Companies**<br />
  [General Resolution 5614/2024](https://www.argentina.gob.ar/normativa/nacional/resoluci%C3%B3n-5614-2024-407183) requires large companies to itemize VAT and other indirect national taxes on electronic invoices, providing greater transparency to consumers about the tax components of their purchases.
</Update>

<Update label="Nov 2024" tags={["B2B", "B2G", "B2C"]}>
  **AFIP Renamed to ARCA**<br />
  The tax authority AFIP (Administración Federal de Ingresos Públicos) was officially renamed to [ARCA (Agencia de Recaudación y Control Aduanero)](https://www.afip.gob.ar) - Argentine Customs and Revenue Control Agency. All e-invoicing systems and requirements continue under the new name.
</Update>

<Update label="18 Dec 2024" tags={["B2B", "B2C"]}>
  **Foreign Currency E-Invoicing Procedures**<br />
  [General Resolution 5616/2024](https://www.globalvatcompliance.com/globalvatnews/argentina-e-invoicing-procedures/) published, mandating that exchange rate information must be included in electronic invoices for transactions conducted in foreign currencies. Implementation occurs in phases through April 2025.
</Update>

## Historical milestones

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="2019: Mandatory for All Including Freelancers">
    Since April 2019, electronic invoicing became mandatory for all companies including freelancers and self-employed individuals in Argentina, completing the universal adoption of the e-invoicing system.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="2015: Widespread E-Invoicing Implementation">
    **April 2015**: [General Resolution 3749](https://edicomgroup.com/blog/electronic-invoice-argentina) came into effect, implementing widespread e-invoicing by mandating the technology for all VAT-registered taxpayers.

    **July 2015**: Electronic invoicing became compulsory for all registered VAT payers in Argentina, marking a major expansion of the system.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="2011: Web Service V1 Launch">
    **January 2011**: Direct sales made through the Internet became required to use the Electronic Invoice regime. All suppliers to the National Government were also required to use the Electronic Invoice scheme. RG 2485 V.1 became effective.

    **March 2011**: [RG 3066](https://edicomgroup.com/blog/electronic-invoice-argentina) became effective, introducing changes to export web services.

    **July 2011**: [Web Service V1 (wsfev1)](https://www.afip.gob.ar/ws/documentacion/ws-factura-electronica.asp) replaced the previous WSFE version, establishing the modern web service infrastructure still in use today.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="2010: Key Resolutions for Market Segments">
    **January 2010**: [RG 2758](https://userapps.support.sap.com/sap/support/knowledge/en/2370720) for export invoices published, establishing the framework for electronic export documentation.

    **September 2010**: [RG 2904](https://userapps.support.sap.com/sap/support/knowledge/en/2370724) issued for the domestic market, creating standardized e-invoicing procedures for domestic transactions with item-level detail.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="2008-2009: Gradual Expansion">
    **August 2008**: [General Resolution 2485](https://userapps.support.sap.com/sap/support/knowledge/en/2370680) published, establishing updated regulations for new activities and applications, expanding e-invoicing requirements to additional sectors.

    **January 2009**: Electronic invoicing became mandatory for tolling, IT, advertising, and professional services companies with turnover over $600,000 (ARS), as established in General Resolution 2485.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="2002-2006: Early Development">
    **2002**: AFIP introduced the electronic invoice in Argentina as a duplicate voucher through [General Resolution 1361](https://edicomgroup.com/blog/electronic-invoice-argentina), marking the beginning of digital invoicing in the country.

    **2005**: General Resolution 1956 established the electronic invoice issuance and storage system, creating the technical foundation for the clearance model.

    **2006**: [General Resolution 2177](https://edicomgroup.com/blog/electronic-invoice-argentina) established mandatory electronic invoicing gradually for initial groups of taxpayers, including telephone companies, Internet and cable TV operators, basic services providers, prepaid medicine companies, security services, and cleaning companies.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
