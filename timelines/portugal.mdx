<Update label="1 Jan 2027" tags={["B2B, B2G"]}>
  **Qualified Electronic Signature**<br />
  Companies in Portugal that issue PDF invoices will be required to include a Qualified Electronic Signature (QES) that confirms the authenticity and integrity of the document.
</Update>



<Update label="Future (tbd)" tags={["B2B", "EU"]}>
  **VAT in the Digital Age (ViDA)**<br />
  Portugal may extend real-time e-reporting or e-invoicing to all domestic and cross-border sales as part of the EU VAT in the Digital Age (ViDA) initiative.
</Update>
