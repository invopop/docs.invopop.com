## Timeline

Current and upcoming regulation for Portugal.

<Update label="Now" tags={["B2B", "B2G", "B2C"]}>
  **E-invoicing and e-reporting systems enforced to date**<br />
  Existing systems enforced in Portugal regarding e-invocing, e-reporting and fiscalization.

  | System        | Description  | Invopop Coverage |
  |---------------|---------------|---------------|
  | QR/ATCUD | Invoices are required to have an ATCUD and QR Code | Yes |
  | SAF-T | E-reporting required either in real-time or via SAF-T reports | Yes |
  | CUIS-PT | B2G XML invoice format | No |

<Update label="1 Jan 2027" tags={["B2B", "B2C"]}>
  **Qualified Electronic Signature**<br />
  Companies in Portugal that issue PDF invoices will be required to include a Qualified Electronic Signature (QES) that confirms the authenticity and integrity of the document.
</Update>

<Update label="Future (tbd)" tags={["B2B", "EU"]}>
  **VAT in the Digital Age (ViDA)**<br />
  Portugal may extend real-time e-reporting or e-invoicing to all domestic and cross-border sales as part of the EU VAT in the Digital Age (ViDA) initiative.
</Update>
