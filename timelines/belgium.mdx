<Update label="1 Jan 2026" tags={["B2B"]}>
  **EN 16931 e-invoicing enforced for B2B Companies**<br />
All invoices issued, delivered or transmitted on or after this date must be in a structured electronic format. Even though legislation doesn't mention Peppol, in practice it's the only compliant network in Belgium.

    | System        | Description  | Invopop Coverage |
  |---------------|---------------|---------------|
  | [Peppol](#peppol) | Peppol is a standardized European network that enables the secure exchange of electronic business documents—such as invoices—between organizations using interoperable formats and certified access points. | Yes |

</Update>

<Update label="1 Jan 2028" tags={["e-reporting"]}>
  **E-reporting roll-out**<br />
  Belgium plans to complement the e-invoicing mandate with an electronic reporting (e-reporting) regime enabling near-live data transmission.
</Update>

<Update label="Future (tbd)" tags={["B2B", "EU"]}>
  **VAT in the Digital Age (ViDA)**<br />
  Belgium may extend real-time e-reporting or e-invoicing to all domestic and cross-border sales as part of the EU **VAT in the Digital Age (ViDA)** initiative.
</Update>
