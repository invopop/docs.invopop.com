<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I add attachments to an e-invoice?">
    Yes. The structured "Zalacznik" (attachment) is allowed for tax-relevant data on  quantities and unit prices. The feature is aimed at technical transactions in the energy sector, but any company can apply to use them.

    Each attachment is limited to 3 MB, and should not include any non-tax related materials (such as contracts, manuals, advertising).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will B2C transactions need to be reported in KSeF?">
    No. B2C invoices in KSeF are optional.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What KSeF applications are available? ">
    - [<u>KSeF Taxpayer Application</u>](https://ksef.podatki.gov.pl/aplikacja-podatnika-ksef-i-inne-narzedzia/bezplatne-narzedzie-wersja-produkcyjna/) – the main portal with all functionalities. 
    - [<u>KSeF Mobile Application</u>](https://ksef.podatki.gov.pl/aplikacja-podatnika-ksef-i-inne-narzedzia/aplikacja-mobilna-ksef/) – a mobile app.
    - [<u>e-mikrofirma</u>](https://login.mf.gov.pl/Account/Select?ReturnUrl=%2Fconnect%2Fauthorize%2Fcallback%3Fclient_id%3D09e2877e-702a-4e27-8818-9472ca964c0a%26redirect_uri%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Furzadskarbowy.gov.pl%26response_type%3Dcode%26scope%3Dprofile%2520openid%2520DataHub.UserAccess%2520eFormsClientApi.UserAccess%2520ChatApi.UserAccess%2520offline_access%26state%3D8f196c7682654d0da49b42bfc9045e51%26code_challenge%3DSIwxyxXJpAzUgzrMW96UIH9vNc6P0-dkdc7l482AWQA%26code_challenge_method%3DS256) – a portal aimed at micro-entrepreneurs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What types of invoices are supported in Invopop's KSeF Integration?">
    We currently support Standard Invoices (VAT) and Credit Notes (KOR). We add support for more types as required by our customers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I don't have presence in Poland in order to obtain an Issuer NIP or Token">
    In special cases we facilitate test NIPs and tokens to our clients. Get in touch with the [support team](mailto:support@invopop.com) if you require credentials for testing.
  </Accordion>
