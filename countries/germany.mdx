## Executive summary

Germany is transitioning to mandatory e-invoicing for B2B and B2G transactions. The country supports multiple formats including **XRechnung** and **ZUGFeRD**, and is increasingly adopting the **Peppol** network for electronic invoice exchange, particularly for public sector transactions.
