## Executive summary

Poland operates a mandatory electronic invoicing system through **KSeF (Krajowy System e-Faktur)**, managed by the **Ministry of Finance**. The system requires businesses to issue structured electronic invoices for B2B transactions, ensuring real-time tax compliance and validation.
