## Executive summary

Italy operates a mandatory electronic invoicing system through **FatturaPA** for B2B and B2G transactions, managed by the **Sistema di Interscambio (SDI)**. All invoices must be transmitted through SDI in XML format, ensuring real-time tax compliance and validation.
