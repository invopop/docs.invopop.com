## Executive summary

Singapore operates a nationwide e-invoicing network called **InvoiceNow**, built on the **Peppol** framework and managed by the **Infocomm Media Development Authority (IMDA)**. The system enables businesses to exchange electronic invoices securely and efficiently for B2B and B2G transactions.
