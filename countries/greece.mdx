## Executive summary

Greece operates a comprehensive electronic invoicing system through **myDATA**, managed by the **Independent Authority for Public Revenue (AADE)**. The system requires real-time submission of invoice data for B2B, B2C, and B2G transactions, ensuring tax compliance and transparency.
