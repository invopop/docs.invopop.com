## Invoicing in Brazil

<AccordionGroup>
  Brazil operates one of the world's most complex electronic invoicing systems through **SEFAZ (Secretaria da Fazenda)**. The **NF-e (Nota Fiscal Eletrônica)** system requires businesses to issue electronic invoices for B2B, B2C, and B2G transactions, with real-time validation and authorization from tax authorities.
